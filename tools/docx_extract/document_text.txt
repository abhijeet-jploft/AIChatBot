1 Overview:
The objective of this project is to build a fully intelligent AI-powered Website Sales Agent that functions as a digital business representative for a company√Ę‚ā¨‚ĄĘs website. The system is not a traditional chatbot or helpdesk assistant. Instead, it is designed to behave like a trained human sales executive who understands the company√Ę‚ā¨‚ĄĘs business, communicates with visitors, guides them, answers their queries, and converts them into customers or qualified leads.
The AI agent will be deployed directly on a website using a lightweight embedded widget and will automatically interact with website visitors in real time. It will greet users, understand their intent, ask discovery questions, recommend services or products, navigate website pages, and assist them through decision-making and checkout or meeting booking.
The system will be powered by a Large Language Model (LLM) and a custom business knowledge training layer. The AI will be trained using the company√Ę‚ā¨‚ĄĘs actual data, including website content, service offerings, pricing structures, documentation, previous proposals, FAQs, and databases. As a result, the agent will provide contextual and business-aware responses rather than generic chatbot replies.
Initially, the product will be built for IT and software development companies (such as agencies offering websites, mobile apps, SaaS platforms, and custom software). In the long term, the system will be released as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform so any business (e-commerce, education, healthcare, consulting, etc.) can create their own branded AI sales representative and deploy it without technical knowledge.
The core purpose of the system is to automate sales conversations, improve user engagement, reduce manual sales workload, increase website conversion rates, and ensure that every visitor is handled instantly 24/7.
2 User Role Overview:
Chatbot (AI Assistant):
The Chatbot acts as an intelligent assistant within the platform, helping users interact with the system more efficiently and understand the insights generated from the data. It assists users in navigating the platform, explaining features, and answering questions related to traffic conditions, road quality, flood risks, and environmental exposure across different corridors or road segments. The chatbot can help users generate reports, interpret analytics, and quickly identify key disruptions or trends within the selected time period. By simplifying access to insights and guiding users through the platform√Ę‚ā¨‚ĄĘs capabilities, the chatbot improves usability and enables decision-makers to extract actionable information from the system more quickly.
Super Admin (Platform Owner):
The Super Admin is the highest authority within the platform and has complete control over the system. This role is responsible for managing the overall platform, including client organizations, users, system configurations, and data operations. The Super Admin can create and manage organizations, assign roles such as Client Owners and Client Users, and control access levels across the platform. They oversee the data ingestion pipeline to ensure that vehicle-collected data such as GPS, sensor, and environmental inputs are being processed correctly. Additionally, the Super Admin manages API access, monitors system performance, configures alert rules, and generates high-level reports. This role ensures the platform operates smoothly, securely, and remains scalable as more organizations and data sources are added.

The Owner shall be able to control which system notifications they receive.
Notification Types
New lead captured
Meeting request received
Training completion
Payment notifications
System alerts
Notification Channels
Email notifications
Dashboard alerts
The Owner may enable or disable specific notification types.
4.8.7 Language Preferences
The Owner may configure preferred languages used by the AI agent.
Features
Select primary language
Enable additional supported languages
The AI agent shall use these preferences when communicating with visitors whenever possible.
4.8.8 Data Management
The Owner shall have basic controls related to stored data.
Features
Request data export (optional)
Remove uploaded knowledge files
Clear historical training data
Data removal actions may require confirmation before execution.
4.8.9 Logout
The Owner shall be able to securely log out from the dashboard.
Logging out will terminate the current session and require authentication for future access.
